About Me



HI! I'm Soon Yuan Chi. I'm majoring in Derpartment of Technology Application and Human Resource Development, with a double major in Program of Learning Science. I have a strong passion for eye-tracking research, programming, and visual design. Exploring eye-tracking technology allows me to understand human behavior and attention, which is crucial for user experience and interaction design. I enjoy programming and use various languages and tools to develop innovative applications. I strive for efficient, user-friendly designs that provide exceptional software experiences. In addition, I have a keen eye for visual design. I love creating captivating visual effects using different techniques and tools. As a student of learning science and technology, I'm detail-oriented, analytical, and a problem-solver. I actively collaborate in team projects, sharing my knowledge and experiences. Outside of academics, I enjoy sports and artistic pursuits to maintain a balanced lifestyle. I look forward to applying my skills and knowledge in my future career and making a positive impact. Thank you!



關於我



大家好！我是孫源智。我主修科技應用與人力資源發展學系，雙主修學習科學學士學位學程。 我對眼動儀研究、程式設計和視覺化設計充滿熱情。通過研究眼動儀技術，我能夠了解人類行為和注意力，這對於使用者體驗和互動設計非常重要。 我喜歡程式設計，使用不同的程式語言和工具開發創新的應用程式。我追求高效、使用者友好的設計，提供卓越的軟體體驗。 此外，我對視覺化設計有敏銳的觀察力。我喜歡運用各種技術和工具創造引人入勝的視覺效果。 除了學術之外，我也熱愛運動和藝術，保持生活的平衡。 我期待將我的技能和知識應用於未來的職業生涯，為世界帶來積極的影響,也希望可以向前輩們學習。雖然我有很多的不足，但相信如果可以幫助，我將獲益良多，謝謝
